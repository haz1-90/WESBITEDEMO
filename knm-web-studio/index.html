What we do

The three parts of a website, in plain terms.

You do not need the technical words. Here is what each part means, and what it does for your business.

The front

Everything your customer sees and reads: the layout, the photos, the colours, and the words that build trust at first glance.

In short: how your website looks and feels.

The engine

The part that works behind the scenes: online payments, bookings, enquiry forms, stock, and records. It does the jobs you would otherwise do by hand.

In short: how your website actually does the work.

Ease of use (UI / UX)

We arrange every page so it is simple to read and quick to act on. Fewer taps, clear buttons, and nothing in the way of a sale or a booking.

In short: making it effortless for your customer.

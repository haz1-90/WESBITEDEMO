Terms & Conditions

Summary of terms

Shipping. Orders are shipped within two working days. Shipping is RM10 for West Malaysia and RM15 for East Malaysia (Sabah, Sarawak, and Labuan), and free on orders over RM250. A tracking number is sent to your WhatsApp once the order has been posted.
Returns. Unworn items with tags attached may be returned within 14 days of delivery. Return postage is borne by the customer unless the item is faulty.
Stock. The quantities shown are updated by us and may differ slightly during busy launches. If a size sells out after you place an order, we will issue a full refund.
Privacy (PDPA). The name, phone number, email address, and delivery address you provide are used only to process and deliver your order, and to contact you about it. We do not sell your data. You may request correction or deletion of your details at any time.
Payment. Payment is processed through the store's own ToyyibPay / FPX account, and funds go directly to the store. We do not store your card or banking details on this site.

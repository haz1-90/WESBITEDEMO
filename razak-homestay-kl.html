House Rules

A few house rules, simply put.

These keep both homes comfortable for every guest and respectful to the residents who live alongside you. By booking, you agree to the points below.

Arrival & departure

  • Check-in is from 3:00 PM; check-out is by 12:00 noon.
  • Earlier arrival or later departure may be arranged in advance, subject to availability.
  • Self check-in details are shared on the day of arrival once your booking is confirmed.

Living considerately

  • The homes are non-smoking throughout, including balconies.
  • Please keep noise low after 10:00 PM out of respect for neighbouring residents.
  • Parties, events, and commercial filming are not permitted.

Guests & visitors

  • Only the number of guests listed in your booking may stay overnight.
  • Day visitors are welcome, but please let the host know in advance.
  • A valid ID is required from the lead guest at booking, in line with building policy.

Safety & care

  • Pets are not allowed unless agreed with the host beforehand.
  • Please switch off air-conditioning and lights when you leave the home.
  • Treat the furnishings and shared facilities as you would your own.

Deposit & damages

  • An RM100 deposit secures your dates and is applied toward your stay.
  • The home is checked after departure; any damage beyond normal use may be charged.
  • Lost access cards or keys are subject to a replacement fee.

Changes & cancellation

  • Let the host know as early as possible if your plans change.
  • Cancellation terms are confirmed in writing on WhatsApp at the time of booking.
  • Date changes are accommodated where the calendar allows.
